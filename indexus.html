Ship Chandler Bandeirantes LTDA is a very traditional general ship supplier operating in Santos for more than 5 decades.

Working with the supply of all types of products, we have established ourselves over the years with the general recognition of our customers, regarding the quality of our services.

Having started as a small wood supplier in 1977, we worked hard to achieve the possibility of extending our activities, becoming during the first 5 years of our existence the largest supplier of wood and woodworking material in Santos.
